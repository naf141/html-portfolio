The Best Movies According to Nichole

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-verse

This is in one of my top 3 movies because it displays really cool animation, has a great story-line, and involves one of my favorite superhero. The general plot of this movie follows Miles Morales and his origin story into becoming the new Spider-Man.

Kill Bill - both volumes

The general vibe of these movies are just great for action and a basic revenge plot. I watched it entirely too young but that's also probably why I'm attached to it.

Lord of The Rings - all movies

The reasoning should be obvious: great story, fleshed-out characters, and just overall great fantasy.
